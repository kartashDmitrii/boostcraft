Battle for Azeroth Season 4 Mythic Plus Dungeons Boost Carry

Completing Mythic+ Dungeon Run will grant you access to 2 chests: End of Dungeon Chest in the end of mythic+ dungeon run witch MAY contain reward for you and one Weekly Challenger's Chest in your faction's capital city which unlocks next reset and contains one guaranteed item. In BFA Season 4 these items have different item levels (which depend on a keystone passed) from 435 ilvl item from Mythic+ 2 end of dungeon chest up to 475 ilvl item from Mythic+ 15 level weekly chest, which makes mythic+ 15 dungeon gear on par with Ny'alotha Mythic raiding gear. These items can also be corrupted and socketed.

End of Dungeon chest contains 2 item pieces of appropriate item level for a group by default and if you beat the dungeon within the timer, your group will get third item as a bonus. Additionally to that, you will get one guaranteed piece of loot in your faction's weekly chest next week.
